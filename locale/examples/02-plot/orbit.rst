Orbiting
~~~~~~~~~

Orbit around a scene.

NOTE: using ``p.open_movie('orbit.mp4')`` is better than
``p.open_gif('orbit.gif')``

For orbiting to work you first have to show the scene and leave the plotter open
with ``.show(auto_close=False)``.
