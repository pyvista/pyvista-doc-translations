Blurring
~~~~~~~~
This example shows how you can use :func:`add_blurring
<pyvista.Plotter.add_blurring>` to blur a plot, or use
:func:`enable_depth_of_field <pyvista.Plotter.enable_depth_of_field>`
to highlight part of your plot.
